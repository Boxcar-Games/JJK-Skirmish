Mai Zenin

Mai Zenin is a student at Kyoto High and a member of the Zenin clan. Mai is also Maki's younger sister. Mai relies upon her firearms to deal most of her damage and she can be quite potent with her rapid fire ability. She can also surprise her enemies by creating a bullet out of cursed energy directly in the chamber of her revolver
