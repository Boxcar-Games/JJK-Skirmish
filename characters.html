Yuji Itadori

With a high stun build, Yuji can disable his opponents with divergent fist or black flash, before finishing them off with Dismantle. Being Sukuna's vessel also grants him access to both his own domain and sukunas

    • Health = 200
    • Attack = 40
    • Defense = 15
    • Speed = 50
    • Cursed Energy = 1350

      • Ability Type = Cursed Technique
      • Damage = 80
      • Stun = 5

      • Stun = 60
      • Cost = 250
